Our Story

From a Single Shovel to a Full-Service Team

GreenScape was founded in 2006 by Marcus Green — a horticulturalist with a passion for turning neglected yards into thriving outdoor sanctuaries. What started as weekend lawn mowing with a pickup truck has grown into a 50-person team serving thousands of residential and commercial clients.

Our philosophy has always been the same: work with nature, not against it. Every design we create balances aesthetics with ecology, using native plants, smart irrigation and organic treatments wherever possible.
