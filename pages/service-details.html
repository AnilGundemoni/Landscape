What's Included

Our lawn care programme is a comprehensive, season-long treatment plan designed to produce consistently healthy, beautiful turf. We use professional-grade equipment and eco-friendly products on every visit.

  • Precision mowing at the correct seasonal height
  • Clean edging along all paths, driveways and bed borders
  • Power trimming around obstacles and structures
  • Clipping collection and site cleanup after each visit
  • Seasonal fertilization programme (4 applications)
  • Broadleaf weed control treatments (2 per season)
  • Spring aeration and overseeding (Professional plan+)
  • Detailed visit report sent via app or email

Our Process

Free On-Site Assessment

We visit your property, measure the turf area, assess soil condition and discuss your goals. No obligation, no pressure.

Custom Care Plan

We create a tailored schedule and product recommendation based on your grass type, soil test results, and local conditions.

Regular Scheduled Visits

Your dedicated crew arrives on schedule. You receive an arrival notification and post-visit report after every service.

Seasonal Adjustments

We adapt the programme as seasons change — spring prep, summer maintenance, fall aeration, and winter prep are all included.
