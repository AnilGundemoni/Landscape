FAQ

Common Questions

Can I cancel my plan at any time?+
Yes — monthly plans can be cancelled anytime with 30 days notice. Annual plans include a pro-rated refund after 3 months.
What if I'm not satisfied?+
We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with a visit, we'll return within 48 hours to re-do the work at no charge.
Are your teams insured and certified?+
All crew members are fully insured and background-checked. We carry in general liability insurance. Our arborists are ISA-certified.
Do you work in my area?+
We serve the Greater New York area including Nassau and Suffolk counties. Enter your zip code during booking to confirm coverage.
How does billing work?+
Monthly plans are billed automatically on the 1st of each month. Annual plans are billed upfront with a 20% discount applied.
